About Me

Hi, I'm Janvi, a graduate researcher with Masters degree in Computer Science with a specialisation in Information Technology and Society. My broad research interests includes : Social Psychology, Multi-Agent Systems, Game Theory, Social Sciences, and Philosophy.

I believe that all these domains are so dependent on each other. Hence, multidisciplinary approach is required to solve the problems. Systems science and game theory can contribute to approach towards various psychological and social science problems. I would share some of my previous work and writings here.
